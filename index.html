Welcome to Football Game!

How to Play

  • Select Player: Click on any blue player or press C to cycle
  • Move Player: Use arrow buttons or WASD keys
  • Sprint: Hold Shift while moving or click Sprint button
  • Pass Ball: Press P or click Pass button when you have the ball
  • Shoot: Press S or click Shoot button when near opponent's goal
  • Score Goals: Get the ball into the red team's goal
  • Space Bar: Start/Pause game
  • Ctrl+R: Reset game
